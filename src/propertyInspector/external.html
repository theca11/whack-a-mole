WHACK-A-MOLE
Game Instructions

The goal is to whack as many moles as possible before the time runs out.

Regular moles add 1 to the score, shy moles add 2 since they're harder to spot, and miner moles substract 1 because they have protective helmets! In endless mode the same applies when adding seconds to the timer.

> Whack: press the key with the mole to whack it
> Start: press to start game, long press to cancel an ongoing game
> Difficulty/timer: press to change game length, long press to change game difficulty
> Score: press to toggle between last and top score for the current difficulty/length

Custom Difficulty Level Settings
